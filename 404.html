Selected works · 2014 – 2026

Fifteen projects across architecture, infrastructure and urban regeneration.

A working portfolio spanning rammed-earth community building in northwest Mexico, adaptive reuse in heritage districts, urban masterplans, mixed-use development, hospitality, residential, and infrastructure competitions across four continents. From the bachelor thesis in Prague to current work in Culiacán, Madrid, and Queensland — the same conviction at different scales.

01 · 2026 · Adaptive reuse · Cultural

Cultural Center Cal y Mayor

Culiacán, Sinaloa · México · In progress

Renovation and adaptive reuse of a 1953 Modernist residence into a public cultural centre. The 1,700 m² programme distributes between an underground exhibition hall, restored upper-level civic rooms, and a botanical and sculpture garden on the acquired neighbouring plot. The intervention preserves the original brick fabric while introducing a celosía-screened envelope and a planted rooftop terrace.

03 · 2020 · Master plan · Master's thesis

NATURA Masterplan

Culiacán · México

A 95-hectare master plan adjacent to Culiacán airport, converting agricultural land into a mixed-use urban district. The orthogonal grid is interrupted by a single waving bicycle and pedestrian promenade that connects all districts and absorbs the seasonal flooding regime as bioswale infrastructure.
